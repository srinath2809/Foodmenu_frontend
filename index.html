when did chicken fried appear in the Great Britain?

The Scottish were the first to have been recorded as deep frying their chicken in fat with breadcrumbs and seasonings, as evidenced by a recipe in an 1747 cookbook by Hannah Glasse and a 1773 diary entry describing fried chicken on the Isle of Skye.
